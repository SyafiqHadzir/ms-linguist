## Hikayat Melayu (Malay Tales)

Hikayat comes from the Arabic word ‘حکا’ which means ‘to tell’, while ‘حکاية’ means ‘story’ (or 'tale').[^1]

[^1]: Awang, H. (1986) _Glosari Mini Kesusasteraan_. Petaling Jaya: Fajar Bakti.
